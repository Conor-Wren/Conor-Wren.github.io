Mathematics Made Easy

Workshop Background

This workshop is designed for those undertaking higher level mathemaitcs. It is desiged to comprehensively prepare students on several topics on the syllabus. The work shops enables students to understand the topics conceptually and develops both their confidence and abilty to critically analyse problems.

The workshop is run by expert mathematics teachers who are dedicated to the success of their students. All resources used throughout the workshop such as the Powerpoint presentations, notes, question and answer sheets used are made available to those who attend the workshop electronically. Students will engage in several activities throughout the workshop, from team problem solving exercises to indiviual minature assessments.

Workshop Topics

  • Complex Numbers
  • Differentiation
  • Probability
  • Algebra Skills I
  • Algebra Skills II
  • Sequences and Series
  • Integration
  • Inferential Statistics
  • Co-ordiante geometry of a circle
  • Co-ordinate geometry of a line
